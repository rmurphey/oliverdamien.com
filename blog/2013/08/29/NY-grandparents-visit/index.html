Oliver's New York Grandparents Visit

Oliver’s New York grandparents — Rebecca’s parents — came for a week-long visit a few weeks ago. Oliver got lots of love and cuddles from his grandma and grandpa. We all spent time on the porch together (under the FANS — one of Oliver’s favorite things in the whole world!). They also took Oliver on some walks around the neighborhood.

We all went out to Elmo’s for breakfast one day, to Monuts for breakfast another day, and Nana Taco for lunch another day. Oliver’s grandma took Rebecca’s place at a baby massage class one afternoon; unfortunately, Oliver slept through most of it and then woke up hungry, so we practiced on the plastic baby mannequins instead of on Oliver.

Oliver also got to spend time with his grandma and grandpa when they babysat him for a few hours one morning while we checked out child care options for when I return to work in November, and one evening so Rebecca and I could go out for a nice dinner. He was a very sweet boy for them both times.
