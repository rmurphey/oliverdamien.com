Tall Boy

We saw the pediatrician again today, and Oliver grew two whole inches in just one month! He’s now 23-½ inches long. That means he’s at the 60th percentile for height (he was at the 25th percentile last month). His weight is holding steady at the 25th percentile (10 pounds 14 ounces at this visit). His head circumference increased just a little bit, to the 30th percentile (from the 25th percentile at the last visit). So he’s a tall, lean baby boy!
